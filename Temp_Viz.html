Max Temperature

As expected, the temperature becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator. (0 Deg Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisohere tebds to be warmer at this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
